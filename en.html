AI Solution Business

Providing AI Solutions

StatHack provides AI solutions that help you make better use of your data to improve your results.
We will support our customers from consultation at the stage of considering the introduction of AI technology to development, implementation, and operational support.

StatHack provides visual inspection AI which is capable of high-speed, high-precision visual inspection of products with a small amount of data. AI takes over inspections that used to be performed by human inspectors, thereby improving operational efficiency and reducing costs.

Labor-saving delivery note and invoice processing for the manufacturing industry. Automatically reads paper information, saves labor in accounting work, and moves data to the cloud.

Any data that can be expressed numerically is eligible for an AI solution. Please feel free to contact us to discuss how we can help you solve your problems and make the most of the data you have.

Case

We will show you how AI technology is being used by our clients in their business operations.

example 01

Appearance inspection of metal pipe products

Bushu Kogyo Co.

We have introduced AI to classify good and defective metal pipe products. By replacing a major vendor's inspection AI, we have achieved highly accurate and low-cost AI inspection.

Deliverable 1: Appearance Inspection AI

In addition to the high accuracy of 99.8% inspection, damaged areas are visualized in red so that inspectors can confirm the basis of the AI's judgment.

Deliverable 2. Learning Application

We have provided an application that allows customers to learn AI on their own. This enables customers to inspect new products at no additional cost.

example 02

Inspection of screw fastening of building scaffolds

Takamiya Co.

We have introduced an AI that inspects whether rivets (a type of screw) are fastened to spiders (scaffolds) at construction sites.

Deliverable 1: Appearance Inspection AI

We have created an AI that can detect rivets as small as 3 mm from a spider approximately 4 meters long. Normally, detection is difficult when the overall image is large compared to the target, but our algorithm achieves high accuracy without losing detail information.

Deliverable 2. Appearance Inspection Application

We provided an application for operating the appearance inspection AI from a PC. The application was created with a UI that is easy for inspectors to operate on a touch panel in accordance with the on-site environment.

Key Technologies Enabling AI Solutions

The best AI technology for the task is appropriately selected from StatHack's suite of AI modules.

*This is just one example, there are many other AI technologies available.

Object Detection

Identify the type and location of specific objects in the image. Applications include installing cameras on production lines to count products.

Image Classification

Classifies objects based on image features. It is used for sorting good and bad products using cameras.

OCR

Identifies the type and position of text in an image. It can be used to digitize paper documents and extract specific information.

Anomaly Detection

It identifies normal and abnormal sounds. Microphones are installed in factories to check whether manufacturing equipment is working properly.
