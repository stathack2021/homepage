SERVICE

AI Solution Business

StatHack provides AI solutions that help you make better use of your data to improve your results.
We will support our customers from consultation at the stage of considering the introduction of AI technology to development, implementation, and operational support.

example 01

Computer Vision

CV, Image Analysis

Computer vision is a field that aims to allow computers to process images and videos in a cognitive manner similar to humans. It is a field that has grown most significantly with the development of deep learning technology, and has now matured to the point where it records accuracy that exceeds that of humans in various tasks.

Image Classification

Classification is performed based on images taken by a camera. It is applied to the automatic detection of defective products in manufacturing lines.

Optical Character Recognition

Recognizes and extracts text information contained in images. It has applications such as extracting only the necessary information from a huge amount of documents.

example 02

Natural Language Processing

NLP, Text Analysis

Natural language processing is a field that aims to extract and process information from the language (natural language) that people use in their daily lives by using computers. It has been considered to be a difficult field because it is closely related to advanced human information processing, but in recent years, remarkable progress has been made with the emergence of new models.

Document Retrieval Systems

We build a search system by accurately quantifying document information. This makes it possible to retrieve the necessary document information without depending on the experience of the searcher.

Interactive System

Build a system that automatically responds to text messages. It is applied to automate responses and assist operators in customer support.

example 03

Other Data Analysis

Analysis of all data expressed in numerical form

Not only the above image and text data, but any data that can be expressed in numerical values can be subject to machine learning. We will propose a way to effectively utilize your data, so please feel free to contact us.

Sales optimization through purchasing data analysis

It predicts demand based on data on the number of products sold to date, and uses AI to propose optimal purchasing and inventory.

Prediction using sensors

We build systems that detect abnormalities and predict possible abnormalities that may occur in the next few minutes based on sensor data such as heat sensors in factories.
